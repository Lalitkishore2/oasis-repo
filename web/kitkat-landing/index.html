The Anatomy of a Break

What makes a KitKat so iconic? It is the careful orchestration of three distinct elements, designed to give you the ultimate sensory escape.

Crispy Wafer

Three layers of light, crispy oven-baked wafers, crafted to deliver the signature delicate crunch in every single bite.

Velvet Chocolate

Enrobed in a thick, luxurious coating of sustainably sourced milk chocolate, melting smoothly to coat the crispy core.

The Perfect Snap

Intentionally designed with four individual fingers, made to be snapped, shared, and savored at your own pace.

Our Story

The Ritual of the Break

Since 1935, KitKat has been the companion to moments of pause. We believe that a break isn't just a pause in your day—it's a ritual. It’s the satisfying snap of the chocolate, the light crunch of the wafer, and the momentary escape from the rush of the world. Crafted with carefully selected cocoa and baked to golden perfection, every bar is a celebration of taking a moment for yourself. Enjoying a KitKat is a reminder to slow down, take a deep breath, and appreciate the sweetness in between.
